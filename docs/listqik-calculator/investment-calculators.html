Recommended Purchase Price = Present value of the remaining payment stream at your required yield.

Estimated Remaining Note Balance = Estimated balance implied by the payment stream using the note's contract interest rate.

Purchase Discount = Dollar and percentage discount from the estimated remaining balance.

Gross Cash Spread = Scheduled payments minus purchase price before servicing costs, taxes, defaults, or prepayments.

Estimated ROI = Gross Cash Spread divided by purchase price.

Simplified note pricing for analysis only. Does not model default risk, prepayment, servicing costs, payment timing, or borrower credit quality. Consult qualified professionals before purchasing or selling notes.
